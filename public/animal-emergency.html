Animal Care India emergency team. Call or WhatsApp with your location and photos so responders can reach you faster.

Call Now +91 9315982650 Immediate voice assistance WhatsApp Now +91 9315982650 Send photos & pin location

Tip: Send your GPS location and clear photos of the animal and surroundings for the fastest dispatch.

What To Do If You See An Injured Animal

Follow these steps while the team is on the way.

  1. Stay calm. Approach slowly. Avoid sudden movements that might frighten them.
  2. Assess the situation. From a safe distance, check if the animal is conscious, breathing, and how severe injuries appear.
  3. Secure the area. On roads, try to slow traffic; use hazard markers if you have them.
  4. Contact us immediately. Call or WhatsApp +91 9315982650 with location and photos.
  5. Do not move (unless critical). Unless the animal is in immediate danger, wait for trained staff; moving them incorrectly can worsen injuries.
  6. Provide water if conscious. Use a shallow container. Do not force feed.
